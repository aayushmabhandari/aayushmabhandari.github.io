4 tips to stay sane and get ready for college during summer (Corona edition) Coronavirus has removed many productive things to do during summer. Almost everything has to be done online and that means self discipline levels start hitting record low. Here are 4 ways you can stay functioning: Wake Up before 10 Waking up at 9:59 is fine. Just eat brunch and start doing something so that the whole day does not fly away like sand in a beach. Go for a bike ride/ run at least 3 times in a week. Summer could be a time where relaxing is the priority and that could mean a lot of watching shows and eating chips. So, going outdoors at least 3 times a week is important. With the shortages in bikes, checkout some Develop hobbies that do not require screen time. My personal favorite has been gardening. It saves you money on buying vegetables and I love doing it because of my passion for food. Finally find productive things to do online. Get an online job or an internship. Or try some online courses in Coursera and edX and follow youtube tutorials to build something. Below are some of the projects that I have been building.
